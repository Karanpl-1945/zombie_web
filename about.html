OUR ZOMBIE STORY

How a group of misfits became the undead family

The Origin

It began as most apocalyptic tales do - with a group of individuals who didn't quite fit anywhere else. Drawn together by shared interests, mutual friends, and that inexplicable feeling of finding your people, the Zombies were born. What started as a simple WhatsApp group quickly evolved into something much more profound - a found family of unique personalities who complement each other in the most unexpected ways.

The name "Zombies" perfectly captures our essence - we may appear normal on the surface, but get to know us and you'll discover the wonderfully weird, slightly chaotic, and endlessly entertaining individuals beneath. Our motto, "Sabhi prakar ke janwaar dekhne ka ekmaatra destination," speaks to our celebration of diversity and acceptance of all kinds of personalities within our group.

Our Timeline

Early Days

The first connections were made - a conversation here, a shared class there. The seeds of the Zombies were planted without anyone realizing what would grow.

WhatsApp Group Formation

The official Zombies WhatsApp group was created, originally just for practical purposes but quickly becoming the lifeblood of our social interactions.

First Group Outing

What started as a simple dinner turned into an epic adventure, cementing bonds and establishing traditions that would continue for years to come.

The Inside Jokes Begin

The first of many inside jokes was born, incomprehensible to outsiders but endlessly amusing to us. Our shared language began to develop.

The Zombie Identity

We realized we weren't just a friend group - we were something more unique. The Zombie identity took hold, complete with our signature blend of chaos and camaraderie.

Present Day

Through ups and downs, changes and challenges, the Zombies remain. Still weird, still wonderful, still undeniably us.

Our Philosophy

At our core, the Zombies believe in authenticity above all else. There's no need to pretend or put on airs with this group - we celebrate each other's quirks and imperfections. We're the people you can call at 3am, the ones who will tell you the truth even when it's hard, and the crew that turns even the most mundane activities into adventures.

Our group motto isn't just words - it's a way of life. By embracing all "prakaar ke janwaar," we've created a space where everyone can be their true, unfiltered selves. Whether you're the life of the party or prefer to observe from the sidelines, there's a place for you among the Zombies.
