Meet The Horde

Rupant Dixit

The Kanpur boy with a killer sense of humor who somehow always manages to dodge the "let's visit your home" requests from friends. Rupant is that guy who can lighten up any situation with his witty remarks and infectious laughter.

"My house? Oh, it's under renovation... permanently!"
Special Power: Turning awkward situations into comedy shows
Mystery: The legendary Kanpur home no one has seen
Role in Group: Chief Entertainment Officer

Harsh Agrawalla

The human database of your batch who probably knows more about your academic life than you do. Harsh is the go-to person when you need to know someone's roll number, course schedule, or basically any information that normally would require administrative access.

"Oh, you're in section B? Your roll number must be between 35-70 then."
Special Power: Instant recall of any academic information
Known As: The Walking University Database
Role in Group: The Human Google

Karan Lohiya

The cricket enthusiast with a professional mindset who approaches everything with the seriousness of a test match. While others joke around, Karan is the one calculating the next strategic move. His emotionless exterior hides... well, we're still trying to figure that out.

"Let's focus on the task at hand."
Special Power: Unshakable focus and discipline
Known As: The Stoic Strategist
Role in Group: The Anchor

Vedant Pimparwar

The mechanical department's resident roaster whose one-eye blinking during meals is as legendary as his burns. Vedant can dismantle your ego faster than he can dismantle an engine, all while enjoying his food with that characteristic wink.

"I'm not insulting you, I'm just describing you accurately."
Special Power: Spontaneous combustion (of others' self-esteem)
Signature Move: The Chewing Wink
Role in Group: Chief Roasting Officer

Durba Smriti Saha

The Bengali powerhouse from CSE who brings both intellect and humor to the table. Durba's wit is as sharp as her coding skills, making her the perfect combo of brains and laughs in the Zombies horde.

"In Bengal, we have two passions: food and intellectual debates. Mostly food."
Special Power: Cultural humor with academic undertones
Known As: The Laughing Coder
Role in Group: Cultural Ambassador

Shital Niras

The electrical department's scholarship magnet whose famous "Kuchh bolna tha bol diya" line perfectly captures her unfiltered charm. Shital leads the girls with a mix of maturity and delightful childishness that keeps everyone on their toes.

"Kuchh bolna tha... bol diya!"
Special Power: Attracting scholarship money
Known As: The Electric Queen
Role in Group: Girl Gang Leader

Kanika Kumari

The UP firebrand from civil department who won't tolerate any slander about her homeland. Kanika may be called "chhoti bacchii" by the group, but her temper and spirit are anything but small.

"UP ke baare mein kuchh bhi bola toh... samajh lo!"
Special Power: Instant combustion when UP is mentioned
Known As: Chhoti Bacchii (but don't say it to her face)
Role in Group: The Feisty Protector

Gargee Dhalkari

The visionary head of E-Cell with dreams of becoming a photographer, Gargee has mastered the art of zoning out mid-conversation - probably because she's mentally framing the perfect shot or planning the next big entrepreneurial venture.

"Wait, what were we talking about? I was thinking about this great photo composition..."
Special Power: Seeing the world through a viewfinder
Known As: The Entrepreneurial Artist
Role in Group: The Creative Visionary

Aman Kumar

The Bihari tech enthusiast whose collection of gadgets would put an Apple store to shame. Aman's signature "chasma giraah haii" line is as much a part of his identity as his constant need to flex the latest tech.

"Chasma giraah haii... oh wait, let me take a selfie with my new iPhone first."
Special Power: Acquiring gadgets before they're cool
Signature Move: The Spectacle Drop
Role in Group: Tech Guru & Flex Master

Utkarsh Kesharwani

The Hindi Sahitya Sabha's poetic soul who finds beauty in shayari and life's simple pleasures. Utkarsh's gentle nature and intellectual curiosity make him the group's resident philosopher with exactly zero haters.

"हम 'फ़राज़' शेरों से दिल के ज़ख़्म भरते हैं
क्या करें मसीहा को जब दवा नहीं मालूम ।"
Special Power: Turning everyday moments into poetry
Known As: The Peaceful Poet
Role in Group: Moral Compass & Cultural Custodian

Someshwar

The mysterious member of the Zombies crew whose presence adds an air of intrigue to the group. Someshwar's quiet demeanor hides depths that the group is still exploring.

"Sometimes silence speaks louder than words."
Special Power: Maintaining an aura of mystery
Known As: The Enigma
Role in Group: The Mysterious Element

Peeyush

Hailing from Madhya Pradesh, Peeyush is the quiet observer who understands everything but rarely speaks. His ever-present smile and signature "Aur bhai..." make him unforgettable.

"Aur bhai..."
Special Power: Silent Understanding
Known As: The Smiling Observer
Role in Group: The Silent Strength
